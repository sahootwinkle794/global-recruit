GLOBAL RECRUIT EXPERTS – AMONG THE
TOP RECRUITMENT AGENCIES IN ASIA

In today’s global economy, the need for dependable, skilled, and ethically recruited manpower is more critical than ever. Among the top recruitment agencies in Asia, Global Recruit Experts (GRE) stands out as a trusted partner for international businesses seeking access to the high-quality Asian workforce.

Based in Nepal and India, Global Recruit Experts has built a strong reputation for delivering end-to-end manpower solutions to employers across the Gulf, Europe, and beyond. Our deep understanding of workforce trends, industry needs, and international compliance requirements makes us a reliable partner for companies that need talent without compromise.

Connecting the World to Asia’s Talent :

Asia is home to some of the most hardworking, adaptive, and skilled professionals. The Asian workforce is highly respected for its discipline, strong work ethic, and ability to thrive in fast-paced environments. At GRE, we specialize in channeling this talent to industries that demand both quality and efficiency.

Our candidates undergo strict pre-screening, medical tests, interviews, and trade skill assessments before they are shortlisted — ensuring that only the best make it to your team.

Industries We Serve :

As one of the top recruitment agencies in Asia, GRE provides manpower across a wide range of industries, including:

  • Hospitality & Catering
  • (Chefs, housekeeping, waiters, kitchen helpers)
  • Healthcare
  • (Nurses, caregivers, lab technicians)
  • Construction & Engineering
  • (Masons, electricians, carpenters, civil engineers)
  • Logistics & Transportation
  • (Drivers, warehouse staff, loaders, supervisors)
  • Facility Management Services
  • FMCG, Retail & Customer Service
  • Marine & Shipbuilding
  • Agriculture & Food Production
  • Old Age Home & Domestic Care

We ensure that every candidate is job-ready and aligned with your business needs from day one.

Why Choose Global Recruit Experts?

What sets GRE apart from others in the recruitment space is our commitment to transparency, quality, and speed. We don’t just fill vacancies — we deliver the right talent, at the right time, for the right job. With operations in both Nepal and India, we have access to a large and diverse pool of skilled and unskilled workers.

We handle everything from documentation, visa processing, and compliance, to deployment and after-recruitment support making your hiring process smooth and stress-free.

Empowering Employers with Asian Workforce Excellence :

Global Recruit Experts takes pride in being a responsible bridge between global employers and the Asian workforce. We understand the importance of cultural fit, work ethics, and technical skills when placing manpower overseas. That’s why every recruitment we conduct is tailor-made, ensuring both employer satisfaction and worker success.

Whether you’re a hotel in Doha, a hospital in Germany, or a construction company in the UAE — we deliver manpower that meets international standards and adds real value to your operations.

Conclusion :

If you’re looking to hire talent that is skilled, reliable, and ready to deliver from day one, look no further than Global Recruit Experts. We’re proud to be counted among the top recruitment agencies in Asia, trusted by businesses worldwide to supply quality manpower with professionalism and integrity.

Let GRE help you build a stronger team with the power of the Asian workforce.
